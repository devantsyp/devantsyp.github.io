Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience to start breaking?

Nope! Beginners are welcome. We’ve got resources and members ready to help you learn step by step.

What should I wear to practice?

Comfortable clothes and sneakers with good grip. Think flexibility and freedom of movement.

How often does UNCC Breaking meet?

We typically meet once a week for practice. Check the Events page for the schedule.

Can I just watch before joining in?

Absolutely! Come by, watch, vibe, and join when you’re ready.
