Home

Breakdancing, also known as b-boying, is a style of street dance combining elements from acrobatics, urban dancing, and even martial arts. From its inception in the early 1980s in the Bronx in African-American and Puerto Rican communities, breakdancing has undergone remarkable changes.

What started as a raw and expressive street dance quickly evolved into a global phenomenon - breakdancing has moved from underground battles to international competitions, with events like the Red Bull BC One and the upcoming inclusion in the 2024 Paris Olympics solidifying its place as a respected sport and art form.

About Me
